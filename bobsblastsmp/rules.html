Server Rules

Subject To Change Without Notice

  • Civility and Peace
    • During Peace the following is heavily discouraged or illegal (Theft, Killings, and Griefing)
    • Punishment for these crimes is a Kick / Server Kill to 1 week ban
    • No exploits, or cheats, or hacks, or mods (That provide Unfair Combat or Resource Advantages)
    • Punishments for these crimes are bans for 1 week to 2 months. (Permaban if the Cheat/hack/mod is very severe)
    • All Pvp must start with both parties stating in chat that they agree to the following actions (Pvp)
    • No witch hunting or lynching in game or Irl
  • Dimensions and Resources
    • The End is open from Dec 21st to January 4th and any attempts to enter before or after the specified date will result in a 1 week ban or more.
    • Whoever kills the Ender Dragon is the one to receive the egg on first pick.
    • Offseason a villager will trade end items to players for higher prices to encourage going to the end.
  • Warfare and Hostilities
    • Theft, Killings, and Griefing are only allowed on belligerent factions
    • No Attacking Peaceful Factions if they are not in the war
    • To declare war one must post it in general chat and in Mc Chat to be recognized as a war
  • Factions and Coordination
    • All factions must state their union and name in #factions using the provided list of options.
    • When Claiming land the claim of a single person is always trumped by the claim of a factions or of multiple persons.
    • To claim land one must have built some a building to prove their claim, small structures claim small areas while super structures will claim much more.
    • When declaring war a faction must change its status in #factions to "At war with"

Applying

  1. In order to keep some information about our players we require the following information before you can play:
    • Your Name
    • Minecraft IGN (For Whitelisting)
    • Point of Contact (matrix, discord, email, etc.)
  2. Once you have the above information, send them to server administration and kindly wait until they have time to process your application
  3. "After you have been whitelisted you can now follow the next steps!"
